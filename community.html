Diversity In Digital

Diversity in Digital is a grassroots group of employees from across the organization who are passionate about helping the company achieve a more diverse and inclusive workforce. Their mission is to partner with leadership and employees to help the company enact its value of cultivating a diverse workforce, with focus on race, age, differently-abled, and LGBTQ identities and their intersections.

The areas of focus for this task force are:
    * Increasing recruitment of diverse talent
    * Nurturing an inclusive environment that drives invention and retention
    * Ensuring career advancement opportunities for employees of all backgrounds

10% Commitment Agreement
    This an agreement that employees in Chief Technology Officer Nick Rockwell's reporting structure who participate in the Diversity in Digital task force are permitted to spend up to 10% of their working hours each week devoted to Diversity in Digital initiatives provided that the participant expectations (as outlined in the document) are met.


Find out more about Diversity in Digital’s initiatives here.

“Being involved in groups that advocate for higher diversity and more transparency on our efforts as an organization on this topic [diversity] meant that I can meet people and work with people who are more relentless with desiring change and taking actions. I think that’s the benefit with me personally is to be able to develop allies with these type of people.”

- Robin Lee, Senior Analyst, Subscriber Reporting and Analytics



Women In Tech

Women in Tech is a task force that empowers to create and drive initiatives that will increase the number of women in our technology department.

The areas of focus for this task force are:
    * Recruiting and hiring high quality talent, with an emphasis on identifying women in technology
    * Retaining, supporting and promoting an environment where female talent thrives
    * Strengthening the internal pipeline of women into leadership positions

Babel

What is Babel?
    Babel is a database of the many languages spoken by Times employees and the employees who speak them, available to all at the company, as a way to help fellow employees on the business side and in the newsroom.

What is the purpose of Babel?
    This database is meant for smaller tasks, requiring less than 15 minutes of the speaker's time.
